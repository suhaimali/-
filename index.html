About Us

Since its inception in 1944, Sullamussalam Arabic College has been a beacon of academic excellence in Malabar. With a rich legacy spanning over eight decades, the college is renowned for its value-based education, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and commitment to holistic development. Founded by visionaries like N.V. Abdussalam Moulavi, SSAC continues to empower students from marginalized communities, fostering knowledge, character, and societal impact.
